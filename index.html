Our Brand Story

At Lume & Aura, we believe that scents have the power to transform a space and a mood. Born from a love of elegance and serenity, our candles are hand-poured using eco-friendly soy wax and infused with carefully curated aromas.

Each fragrance is inspired by nature and designed to bring a touch of calm, clarity, or comfort to your everyday life. Whether you're setting the mood for a cozy evening or creating a mindful morning routine, Lume & Aura is your partner in crafting beautiful, aromatic moments.
